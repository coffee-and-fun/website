Instant Incognito

Instant Incognito automatically opens specific websites in incognito mode for enhanced privacy.
Available as an extension for Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari, it ensures your browsing remains private and secure.

Frequently asked questions

What does Instant incognito do?

Picture this: You have a secret clubhouse where no one can see what you’re up to, right? 🏠✨ Instant Incognito creates something like that on your computer! It automatically opens certain websites in incognito mode.

Do you track or store my data?

We're like the friend who never blabs your secrets. 🤫 We believe what you do online is your business, and your business alone.

That’s why:

Zero Tracking: We don't keep tabs on where you go or what you do. No tracking means no tracking, period! 🚫

Data? What Data?: Since we don’t store data, there's nothing to see here – like a diary with invisible ink! 📓✨

Permissions used.

🌐 webNavigation

Think of 'webNavigation' like having a smart compass that knows when you're ready to set sail to a new web page. When you click a link or type in a new address, this permission helps our app to see that you're heading somewhere new. It doesn't peek at what you're doing or where you're going; it just makes sure that if you're visiting one of those secret clubhouse sites, the app switches you to incognito mode faster than a superhero can change their outfit! ⚡

🔗 tabs

The 'tabs' permission is like being able to teleport your stuff from one room to another. This lets our app move your current webpage into a private incognito tab if needed, or open a new one without you having to lift a finger. It's like having a magic wand to open, close, and switch between tabs, all while keeping your secret missions... well, secret! 🧙‍♂️✨ 💾 storage

And for 'storage', imagine having an invisible backpack where you can keep all your favorite app settings and tools. This permission lets our app remember how you've set things up so that everything is just the way you like it every time you use it. It's your personal preference locker, and only you have the key. No one else can peek inside – it's just for your app to make sure your adventure is ready to go whenever you are! 🎒🔐
