That’s why:

Zero Tracking: We don't keep tabs on where you go or what you do. No tracking means no tracking, period! 🚫

Data? What Data?: Since we don’t store data, there's nothing to see here – like a diary with invisible ink! 📓✨

Permissions used.

🌐 webNavigation

Think of 'webNavigation' like having a smart compass that knows when you're ready to set sail to a new web page. When you click a link or type in a new address, this permission helps our app to see that you're heading somewhere new. It doesn't peek at what you're doing or where you're going; it just makes sure that if you're visiting one of those secret clubhouse sites, the app switches you to incognito mode faster than a superhero can change their outfit! ⚡

🔗 tabs

The 'tabs' permission is like being able to teleport your stuff from one room to another. This lets our app move your current webpage into a private incognito tab if needed, or open a new one without you having to lift a finger. It's like having a magic wand to open, close, and switch between tabs, all while keeping your secret missions... well, secret! 🧙‍♂️✨ 💾 storage

And for 'storage', imagine having an invisible backpack where you can keep all your favorite app settings and tools. This permission lets our app remember how you've set things up so that everything is just the way you like it every time you use it. It's your personal preference locker, and only you have the key. No one else can peek inside – it's just for your app to make sure your adventure is ready to go whenever you are! 🎒🔐

About Mystery Rank

In Mystery Rank, you contribute to earn a spot in different categories. The more you give, the higher your rank. But here’s the catch—no one knows how much anyone else has paid. It’s this mystery that makes the competition even more exciting.

Terms and Refunds for Mystery Rank

One-Time Contribution

When you contribute to Mystery Rank, it’s a one-time payment that secures your spot in the credits. Please keep in mind that once the payment is made, it’s final—there are no refunds or changes. We want to keep the process fair and transparent for everyone involved.

How Your Rank is Determined

Your rank within your chosen category is based on how much you contribute. The more you contribute, the higher your rank will be. However, the amount you’ve paid, and what others have paid, will remain private. This adds a layer of mystery and excitement to the competition.

When Your Credit Appears

After your payment is confirmed, your credit will be posted within 12 hours. We work hard to ensure that everyone’s contributions are recognized promptly. If for some reason there’s a delay, we’ll do our best to keep you informed.

Handling Chargebacks

If a chargeback occurs, your credit will be immediately removed from the list, and unfortunately, you’ll be blocked from participating in Mystery Rank in the future. We do this to protect the integrity of the rankings and ensure fairness for all contributors.

Updating Your Listing or Link

We understand that sometimes things change. If you need to update your listing or link after it’s been posted, just send us an email at hello@coffeeandfun.com . We’re happy to make one-time updates to ensure your information is correct.

Need Help or Have Questions?

If you have any questions about the process, or if something isn’t clear, please don’t hesitate to reach out. We’re here to help and want to make sure your experience with Mystery Rank is as smooth and enjoyable as possible.
