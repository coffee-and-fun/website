Create Your Own Account

One day, I was at Kroger picking up some mouthwash, and when I got to the checkout, I realized I’d forgotten my membership card. Without it, the mouthwash was $12, but with the membership, it was only $7. Five bucks might not sound like much, but it adds up!

That’s when I decided to create a simple way to help in situations like this. Now, if you forget your card, you can quickly pull up the barcode and still get the discounts.

It’s always a good idea to create your own account with stores so you can earn rewards personally, but this is a perfect backup for those moments when life happens.

Next time you’re at the counter without your card, don’t stress—just pull this up and save!
