Whether you forgot your password or just want to change it, this guide covers both. We'll also show you how to sign out of every device connected to your account.

---

## Changing Your Password

Use this if you can still log in.

1. Go to [myaccount.google.com](https://myaccount.google.com).
2. Click **Security** in the left menu.
3. Under **How you sign in to Google**, click **Password**.
4. Enter your current password.
5. Type your new password, confirm it, and click **Change Password**.

Done. Your password is updated.

---

## Resetting a Forgotten Password

Use this if you're locked out.

1. Go to [accounts.google.com/signin/recovery](https://accounts.google.com/signin/recovery).
2. Enter your Gmail address and click **Next**.
3. Click **Try another way** if the first option doesn't work for you.
4. Google will send a verification code to your recovery phone or email. Enter it.
5. Choose a new password and confirm it.

Make sure your recovery phone number and email are up to date. You can check at [myaccount.google.com/security](https://myaccount.google.com/security).

---

## Signing Out of All Devices

After resetting your password, you'll want to make sure no one else still has access.

### The Quick Way

Changing your password (above) automatically signs you out of most devices and apps. You'll need to sign back in everywhere with your new password.

### Remove Specific Devices

If you want to see exactly what's connected and remove devices individually:

1. Go to [myaccount.google.com/security](https://myaccount.google.com/security).
2. Scroll to **Your devices** and click **Manage all devices**.
3. Click on any device you don't recognise or no longer use.
4. Click **Sign out**.

### Sign Out of Other Browsers

If you're logged into Gmail on a shared computer:

1. Open [Gmail](https://mail.google.com) in your browser.
2. Scroll to the very bottom of your inbox. In the bottom right corner, click **Details**.
3. Click **Sign out all other web sessions**.

This only affects browser sessions, not the Gmail app on phones.

## Quick Reference

| I want to... | What to do |
|---|---|
| Change my password | myaccount.google.com > Security > Password |
| Reset a forgotten password | [accounts.google.com/signin/recovery](https://accounts.google.com/signin/recovery) |
| Sign out of all devices | Change your password (fastest way) |
| Remove a specific device | myaccount.google.com > Security > Your devices |
| Sign out of other browsers | Gmail > bottom right > Details > Sign out all |
